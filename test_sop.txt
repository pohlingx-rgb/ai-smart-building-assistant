AHU Preventive Maintenance Procedure

1. Check filters monthly.
2. Clean cooling coils quarterly.
3. Inspect belts every month.
4. Verify motor operation quarterly.

Emergency Response Procedure

1. Notify Facilities Manager.
2. Isolate affected equipment.
3. Escalate urgent incidents immediately.
